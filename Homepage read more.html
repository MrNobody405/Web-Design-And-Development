ABOUT US
Our basketball team has been established in APU for over 5 years with 100+ active members that are having training every week to enhance the physical and mental capacity to contribute to our university by participating in evens such as friendly competition between universities, state level competition, basketball day event, etc.We are still actively recruiting for new members to join our family and improve your experience in out university!

We train and play in 2 gyms, St. David’s in Artane and St. Fintan’s in Sutton. Our basketball elite teams play their home matches in the IWA gym in Clontarf on Saturday nights from September to April, where there is spectator seating for all club members to relax, enjoy the games and where families make a social event of the nights, with all the kids able to hang out and watch their beloved game in the company of their team mates.

If you are interested learning the fundamentals of basketball, from the basics of footwork, passing, dribbling and shooting, to more advanced concepts such as defensive zones, body control, the appreciation of space, fast transitions and many more, all in a team environment.

There are no requirements to be a member of our basketball team! However, to actively participate in the training, several requirements needs to be fulfilled such that:

 - Have your own transport
 - Abe to facilitate flexible timing for training
 - Have fun!

With that being said, come join us!
