About Us

We at the APU are enthusiastic about developing and honouring the game of basketball. We captivate hundreds of millions of spectators worldwide via the intensity of the game and the incredible athleticism of our athletes.

While Basketball Operations oversees the league's on-court operations, other divisions deal with a variety of tasks that are essential to its success, including developing relationships with the league's television and digital media partners, creating marketing alliances with some of the most well-known brands in the world, managing NBA merchandise licencing, and handling a variety of other tasks.

We also have a strong commitment to social responsibility off the court. By collaborating with well-known worldwide youth-serving organisations that promote causes linked to education, youth and family development, and health, APU Cares takes the lead in the league's engagement in tackling significant social concerns.
